Let Them Eat Cake

You Are Cordially Invited!

Greetings honored guests! Today, I-your gracious, generous (and slightly dramatic) host- am opening doors to a celebration of sugar, whimsy, and questionable authority.
There is only one rule here. Well... actually... there are several. But they all revolve around cake.

Rule No.1

I will call each gust forward according to age. One bye one. Patiently. Magestically. When your age is spoken, you must answer correctly.

Rule No.2

The correct response? Simply: "Let them eat cake."
Exactly that. No more. No less. No add-ons, emojies, or even nervous giggles.

Rule No.3

Upon perfect recitation, you shall recieve your slice-a fluffy symbol of triumph, etiquette, and delicious compliance.

Rule No. 4

Failure to respond with the sacred phrase results in-gasps
no cake for you!

Rule No.5

Attempts to bribe the host will be met with admiration(but will not get you cake).
